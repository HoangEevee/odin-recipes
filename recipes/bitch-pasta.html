Description

Despite the name pasta, this dish actually uses Udon as its noodle but the sauce is still tomato sauce so it is pretty much still a pasta anyway. The dish is loaded with delicious ingredients such as sliced beef, gyoza, prawn, and brocolli so I sure hope you are hungry!

Ingredients

  • Udon noodle
  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Gyoza
  • Frozen Prawn
  • Tomato
  • Brocolli
  • Shirataki
  • Tofu
  • Ready-made meat sauce

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to the boil and put in your udon noodle.
  2. Into the same pot, put in your beef, gyoza, frozen prawn (you may need to defrost it with a microwave first), brocolli, shirataki, and tofu as well.
  3. While waiting, cut your tomato in half.
  4. After everything have finished cooking, plate them up with noodle on the bottom. Also include the tomato!
  5. Cut open you meat sauce and pour it all over the plate. The more you use, the better.
  6. Finally, microwave your dish for 2 minutes and 16 seconds then you are done!
