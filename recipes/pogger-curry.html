Description

A nice hearty meal of udon curry that will keep you warm through cold day. It is also completely vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Brocolli
  • Udon noodle
  • Sliced cheese
  • Ready-made curry sauce with vegetables

Steps

  1. Prepare a large pot of water and bring it to a boil. While waiting, put in the brocolli and tofu.
  2. When the water start to boil, put in the udon noodle. Make sure that the noodle is completely submerged in water.
  3. After everything have finished cooking, plate them up onto a deep plate.
  4. Cut open the curry sauce and pour it all over.
  5. Finally top up with a single slice of cheese then put the plate into the microwave. Enjoy while it is still hot
